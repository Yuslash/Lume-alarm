# Privacy Policy for Lume Clock

**Effective Date:** July 6, 2026

At Lume Clock, we believe your personal schedule and daily routine are highly confidential. This Privacy Policy outlines our design choices and commitment to maximum user data privacy.

---

## 🔒 1. Absolute Offline Architecture
Lume Clock is built with an **offline-first, zero-telemetry, client-side approach**:
*   **No Accounts:** You do not need to log in, register, or provide an email to use the app.
*   **No Databases:** All configured alarm routines, labels, volume preferences, and selected themes are stored locally on your device.
*   **No Transmissions:** The app never initiates background telemetry, remote network syncs, or diagnostic uploads.

---

## 🛰️ 2. Native System Permissions
To function reliably, our native Android package uses specific Android system permissions. We declare that these permissions are utilized **solely** for user-requested tasks:

1.  **POST_NOTIFICATIONS:** To display alarm notifications, snooze statuses, and daily motivational titles.
2.  **SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM:** To register precise wake-up timers with the Android system, ensuring the alarm sounds at the exact second even when the device goes into sleep mode.
3.  **VIBRATE:** To activate the physical vibration motor on your device during an active alarm alert.

---

## 📝 3. Data Safety & Erasure
Because all data resides directly inside your local application cache:
*   To delete your data, simply navigate to your system settings: *Settings > Apps > Lume Clock > Storage > Clear Data*.
*   Uninstalling the App will completely and immediately delete all saved alarms and custom files from your device.

---

## 📧 4. Legal Compliance
Lume Clock contains zero trackers, making it compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation).
