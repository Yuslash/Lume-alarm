# Play Store Submission Guide

This guide will walk you through uploading **Lume Clock** to the Google Play Console and configuring the store page to ensure swift approval from Google's review team.

---

## 🎨 Asset checklist

When setting up your Play Store listing, prepare the following graphic files:

| Graphic Asset | Required Dimension | Allowed Formats | Important Guidelines |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **App Icon** | 512 x 512 px | 32-bit PNG | Must have transparent backing. |
| **Feature Graphic** | 1024 x 500 px | JPEG or 24-bit PNG | Avoid putting important titles near edges. *Use `/src/assets/images/lume_clock_promo_1783396821396.jpg`.* |
| **Mobile Screens** | Minimum 2 screens | JPEG or PNG | Upload portrait screenshots (9:16) showing the main dashboard and active alarm overlay. |

## 📋 Critical Declarations & Questionnaires

To pass the Play Store automatic review filters, complete your Console forms using these exact answers:

### 1. Data Safety Questionnaire
Lume Clock is private and doesn't collect any user metrics.
*   *Does your app collect or share any of the required user data types?*  
    👉 **No**

### 2. Content Rating (Alcohols, Violence, etc.)
Lume Clock is a simple clocks utility.
*   *App Category:* Utility / Clock
*   *Violence, Sexual, Offensive content, or Gambling:* Select **No** to all questions.
*   *Resulting Rating:* **PEGI 3 / Everyone** (Worldwide).

### 3. Target Audience
*   *What are the target age groups?*  
    👉 Select **13-15**, **16-17**, and **18 and older**.
*   *Could your store listing unintentionally appeal to children under 13?*  
    👉 Select **No** (Design is clean, neutral, and minimalist for adult bedroom aesthetics).

### 4. Alarms Permission declaration
*   *Why does your app use `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM`?*  
    👉 *"Lume Clock is a primary alarm clock application. It utilizes exact alarm triggers to allow users to establish wake-up routines, play synthesized waking chords, and adjust bedside theme backdrops at the exact minute configured by the user. This permission is essential to ensure reliability even during device sleep states."*
